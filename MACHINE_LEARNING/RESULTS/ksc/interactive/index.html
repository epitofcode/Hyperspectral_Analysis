About the KSC Dataset

Kennedy Space Center (KSC) hyperspectral dataset acquired on March 23, 1996 using JPL's AVIRIS sensor.

  • Image Size: 512 x 614 pixels
  • Spectral Bands: 176 bands (after removing water absorption)
  • Spatial Resolution: 18 meters
  • Number of Classes: 13 wetland vegetation types
  • Total Labeled Pixels: 5,211

13 Wetland Vegetation Classes:

  1. Scrub - Coastal scrubland
  2. Willow swamp - Wetland with willow trees
  3. CP hammock - Cabbage palm hammock forest
  4. CP/Oak - Mixed cabbage palm and oak
  5. Slash pine - Pine forest
  6. Oak/Broadleaf - Mixed hardwood forest
  7. Hardwood swamp - Swamp with hardwood trees
  8. Graminoid marsh - Grass-dominated wetland
  9. Spartina marsh - Saltwater marsh grass
  10. Cattail marsh - Freshwater marsh with cattails
  11. Salt marsh - Tidal saltwater marsh
  12. Mud flats - Exposed tidal mud
  13. Water - Open water
